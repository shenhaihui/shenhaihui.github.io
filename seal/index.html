About the Seal

We received this pair of seals from Ms. Wang Xiaoyu as wedding gift. They were made by Xiaoyu's father Mr. WANG Jiadong, an artist with speciality in Chinese calligraphy and seals. We like this pair of seals very much. Nothing can be better than this one as a wedding gift. Thank Xiaoyu and Mr. WANG for such great gift!

About Mr. WANG Jiadong 王家栋

王家栋，字幼君，为王君华先生之子，1953年生于青岛，为藤花书屋传人。 先后从师张叔愚以及吴仓硕先生关门弟子谢静之先生学习书法篆刻，有高深的书法篆刻造诣， 被季羡林誉之为“学者型的金石家”。 又精通音乐，时长将音乐、书法、篆刻融于一体，是为数不多精通音乐、书画、篆刻的艺术大家。 See Bilingual introduction (with traditional Chinese and English).
